Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-28

This Privacy Policy describes how ClawdBot ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when interacting with Meta (Facebook/Instagram) APIs for the Instagram account @tommythebernese.

What data we access

Depending on enabled features and permissions granted, we may access:

  • Instagram profile information (e.g., username, profile metadata)
  • Content insights/metrics (e.g., reach, views, shares, saves)
  • Comments and comment metadata
  • Direct messages (DMs) and message metadata (if messaging automation is enabled)

How we use data

We use data only to operate features requested by the account owner, including:

  • Analytics and reporting to improve content performance
  • Automations such as auto-likes and auto-replies to comments/DMs
  • Operational monitoring and debugging of the integration

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share data with third parties except as required to operate the service (e.g., Meta platforms and hosting providers) or to comply with legal obligations.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the requested functionality and maintain service reliability. Logs may include minimal event metadata for troubleshooting and may be deleted periodically.

Security

Access tokens and secrets are treated as confidential and are stored securely on the server. We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access.

Your choices

  • You can revoke app access at any time via Meta's settings.
  • You can request deletion of any stored data by contacting us (see below).
